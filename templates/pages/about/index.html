We teach.

Each mentor is assigned a small section of 4-5 students to be tutored! If you are a student, join a section and learn more about it here.

We mentor.

We have a tiered system of mentoring opportunities. Senior Mentors write material and provide tips to Junior Mentors on how to teach. All mentors meet up once a week to learn from each other, and meet up another time to teach a section. Our Associate Mentors are either tutors, readers, or other experienced teachers who teach a section without guidance.
