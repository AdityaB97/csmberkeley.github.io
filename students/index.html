For Students
Are you a student at UC Berkeley in CS 61A, 61B or 70, looking for more personalized support? CSM has got you covered.
Support

CSM offers group tutoring to all students in these three classes. Each student will be sorted into one of the timeslots of his or her preference. Each session will be held weekly in either Soda or Cory for one hour from 10-6pm. Mentors will go over a worksheet in section with extra problems to be worked on in groups. Homework, lab, and project help will not be provided during these sessions.

We also work in conjunction with CS370 for one-on-one tutoring. Sign-ups are on a weekly first-come-first serve basis. Homework, lab, and project help will not be provided during these sessions. Check out your respective Piazza to sign up.

Expectations

Students enrolled in group tutoring are eligible for one unit of P/NP credit. Grades are based on attendance. Those attending the session for a grade are required to attend 90% of weekly assigned sessions.

More in-depth expectations.

Application Process

Check out our portal to sign up! Create an account, confirm your email, select a class, and enroll in an empty section.

FAQ
The deadline to sign up has already passed, can I still sign up?

Yes. CSM's biggest priority is reaching out to as many students as we can. However, if you aren't enrolled on Telebears, you will still be required to regularly attend the specific section you are assigned to.

Can I get units for attending a section?

Yes! Each student can receive 1 unit of P/NP credit. We will provide you the appropriate CCN when the time comes. You are not required to enroll. You must attend 90% of assigned weekly sessions in order to pass.

Who can I contact if I have more questions?

Feel free to email us at csmberkeley@gmail.com if you have any questions or concerns!
